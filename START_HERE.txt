🔧 TECHNICAL DETAILS:

Sensors:
  • Wheel Odometry: Position deltas (affected by friction)
  • IMU:            Orientation (noisy during turns)
  • LiDAR:          Ray casting (occluded by obstacles)

Classical EKF:
  • Fixed sensor noise covariances
  • No adaptation to failures
  • Accumulates drift during sensor degradation

AI Trust Model:
  • Rule-based (no deep learning)
  • Sliding window residual analysis
  • Context-aware (friction, angular velocity, obstacles)
  • Real-time computation

Adaptive Fusion:
  • Dynamic measurement covariance scaling
  • Low trust → High covariance → Low fusion weight
  • Preserves EKF mathematical structure
  • Computationally efficient
